# DentalOS Product Strategy

## Why Recall, Follow-Up, and No-Show Reduction First

Dental clinics feel patient leakage every week: overdue hygiene visits, forgotten appointments, unconfirmed bookings, incomplete follow-ups, and dormant patients who need a timely reason to come back. These workflows are operational, measurable, and painful enough for a focused SaaS wedge.

The wedge creates value before DentalOS becomes a full clinic operating system.

## Buyer Pain Points

- Front desk teams spend too much time chasing confirmations.
- No-shows waste chair time and disrupt the day.
- Recall lists are often stale or manually managed.
- Follow-up tasks fall between systems and people.
- Practice owners lack a clear view of patient reactivation progress.

## Initial Target Customer

Small to mid-sized dental clinics with an owner, practice manager, or operations lead who cares about schedule reliability and front-desk efficiency. The ideal pilot clinic has enough patient volume to feel recall and reminder pain but is not yet locked into a large enterprise workflow.

## MVP Scope

- Clinic tenant and team membership foundation.
- Patient import workflow for onboarding a clinic list into recall automation.
- Patient list with recall status and contact preferences.
- Appointment reminder queue and status tracking.
- Recall campaign planning and simple execution states.
- Patient reactivation workspace.
- Activity dashboard for operational follow-up.
- Audit logs for sensitive actions.

## Pilot Offer

Offer a focused pilot around two outcomes:

- Reduce manual reminder and recall work for the front desk.
- Improve visibility into overdue and inactive patient follow-up.

Avoid promising medical outcomes or exaggerated financial results. Measure workflow completion, response rates, no-show trends, and staff time saved.

## Features Not To Build Yet

- Full electronic health records.
- Clinical charting.
- Insurance claims.
- Payment processing.
- Real SMS, email, or WhatsApp sending before approval and audit flows exist.
- Deep PMS integrations before the recall workflow is validated.
- Automated AI actions without human review.
- Multi-location enterprise controls unless pilots demand them.

## Sales-Oriented Value Proposition

DentalOS helps clinics run a tighter patient follow-up operation: know who needs attention, send the right reminders, track what happened, and keep the schedule healthier without asking staff to manage another messy spreadsheet.
